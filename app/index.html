About us

People of color (POC) are grossly under-represented in tech careers.

Currently Black, Latine and Native tech professionals make up less than 5% of the workforce at major tech companies.

POC still face stiff obstacles breaking into tech careers.

ApprentList wants to change that.

Mission

  • To create pathways to good jobs in tech & data science for early-career professionals and students of color through curated paid apprenticeships.

  • We are working towards building a strong, diverse talent pool of minorities in tech & data science through impactful curated apprenticeships.

    •

  • Founders find diverse & driven talent, grow their businesses as they help shape the next generation of minorities in Tech. Apprentices gain valuable skills/mentorship in tech, data science, grow their professional networks and attain a path to good jobs.

  • Our platform connects founders & companies to emerging diverse talent in tech through paid apprenticeships.
